Dr. APJ ABDUL KALAM

The man who inspired many

  • 1931

    Born in Rameswaram,Madras Presidency(now Tamil Nadu)

  • 1941

    At age of 10, turned up as paperboy to add income to his family

  • 1954

    Graduated in Physics from St. Joseph's College, Tiruchirapalli

  • 1958

    Completed Master's in aerospace engineering from Madras Institute of Technology.

  • 1960

    Joined DRDO as scientist in Aeronautical Development Establishment.

  • 1969

    Joined Indian Space Research Organisation(ISRO), worked as project director of India's first Satellite Launch Vehicle

  • 1982

    Rejoined DRDO

    Became Cheif Executive of Integrated Guided Missile Development Programme.

  • 1992

    Became Scientific Adviser to Defence Minister of India

  • 1999

    Became Principal Scientific Adviser to Govenrment of India(Cabinet Rank)

  • 2002

    Elected as 11th President of India

  • Post 2007

    Became visiting professor for various institutes

  • 2012

    Launched a programme for the youth of India called the What Can I Give Movement

  • 2015

    On 27th july 2015 Kalam was confirmed dead of a sudden cardiac arrest at 7:45 p.m after collapsing while giving a lecture on "Creating a Livable Planet Earth" at the Indian Institute of Management Shillong
